About Me

Freelance Flutter Developer — Egypt

I'm Mina Nabil, a Flutter & mobile app developer from Minya, Egypt, currently in my 3rd year of Computer Science at Minia University. I specialize in building production-grade cross-platform applications for Android and iOS using Flutter and Dart — apps that reach real users, not just demos.

My development approach is grounded in Clean Architecture and BLoC/Cubit state management, ensuring every app I ship is scalable, maintainable, and performant. I integrate backends with Firebase (Firestore, FCM, Cloud Functions) and Supabase, handle REST API integration, and manage full Google Play Store deployment from development to release.

Across 3 published apps, I've served 600+ real users, ranging from a university student platform (MN – CS1 with 500+ downloads) to ZEXO, a full commerce and order management system currently in open testing. I'm available for freelance projects and remote collaboration — whether you need a complete mobile app, UI/UX implementation, Firebase integration, or an existing Flutter app refactored and improved.
